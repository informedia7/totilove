Our Commitment

We strive to ensure Totilove meets WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards. We continuously audit and improve the platform to remove barriers and create an inclusive experience for all users.

What We Support

Keyboard navigation Screen readers High contrast mode Scalable text Touch & gestures 10+ languages

Visual Accessibility

  • All colour combinations meet a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1.
  • Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.
  • All images have descriptive alt attributes.
  • Focus indicators are visible on all interactive elements.

Motor & Interaction

  • All functionality is accessible via keyboard without a mouse.
  • Touch targets are at least 44×44 CSS pixels on mobile.
  • No time limits are imposed on form interactions.

Known Limitations

Some older parts of the platform may not yet fully meet our accessibility targets. We are actively working to resolve these. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know.

Feedback & Assistance

If you experience any accessibility barriers or require content in an alternative format, please contact us. We aim to respond to accessibility-related requests within 2 business days.
