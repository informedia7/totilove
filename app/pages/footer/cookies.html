What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your preferences and recognise you between visits. They do not contain executable code and cannot access files on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies — Required for the platform to function. These include session cookies that keep you logged in and security tokens. They cannot be disabled.

Preference Cookies — Remember your language, theme, and display settings so you don't have to re-set them every visit.

Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors use Totilove (e.g. which pages are most visited). Data is aggregated and anonymous. We use these to improve the service.

Marketing Cookies — Only used if you explicitly opt in. These may be used to show relevant promotions.

Cookie Lifespan

  • Session cookies — deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies — stored for up to 12 months unless you clear them sooner.

Managing Your Preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time from our Cookie Preferences page. You can also control cookies through your browser settings — note that disabling essential cookies may affect how the site works.
