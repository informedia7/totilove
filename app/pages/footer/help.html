Getting Started

Creating an account on Totilove is free. Visit the Register page and fill in your details. Once registered, complete your profile to increase your match visibility.

Finding Matches

Use the Search feature to filter by age, location, language, and interests. Our smart matching engine also suggests compatible profiles automatically based on your preferences.

Messaging

You can send a message to any profile you match with. Go to Messages in the navigation bar to view all conversations. All messages are encrypted and private.

Account Settings

Manage your notification preferences, privacy settings, and language in Settings. You can deactivate or delete your account at any time from the Account section.

Billing & Subscriptions

Totilove offers a free tier as well as premium plans. View and manage your subscription under Billing in your account menu. Cancel anytime — no hidden fees.
