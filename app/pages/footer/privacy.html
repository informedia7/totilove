Who We Are

Totilove operates an online dating and social discovery platform available via our website and apps.

The data controller responsible for your personal data is the legal entity operating Totilove in your region. This entity may be identified at checkout, in-app notices, or upon written request.

For privacy-related requests, please use our Contact page or in-app support tools.

As described in our Cookie Policy (including preference controls on the same page).

How We Use Your Information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide matching, profiles, messaging, and notifications

  • Prevent fraud, scams, spam, and abuse

We do not sell your personal information as commonly defined under applicable privacy laws.
